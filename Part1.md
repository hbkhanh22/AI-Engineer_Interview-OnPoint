### 3. What is your most favorite project so far?

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have had the opportunity to work on various projects, which significantly accelerated my skill development. The project I am most proud of is the automated inspection application for network hubs (known as "Group Points"), which I developed during my AI Engineering internship at FPT Telecom.

*   **Project Overview:**
    The goal was to build a mobile application for field technicians to inspect physical network infrastructures (checking cables, screws, connectors, wiring, and identifying pest intrusions). The app automatically evaluates these elements against predefined quality criteria to detect structural anomalies or issues. Automating this process reduced inspection times dramatically compared to manual checks, which were slow and prone to human error due to the extensive checklist.
*   **Technology Stack & Implementation:**
    *   **Data Pipeline:** Gathered raw images from technicians, followed by annotation and data augmentation using Roboflow.
    *   **Computer Vision:** Trained a YOLOv8 object detection model to detect structural components and environmental issues.
    *   **OCR Integration:** Integrated PaddleOCR to extract text labels from equipment tags, matching them against the company's internal databases.
    *   **Deployment:** Packaged the finalized models using Flask and Docker to deploy to production. The solution was successfully integrated into the company's mobile application and is actively used by field technicians.
